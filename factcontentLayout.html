1. Hockey pucks are kept on ice.

Pucks are kept frozen until they are needed in a game. A modern puck is made of rubber, and it becomes flimsy and bounces when it gets warm. Freezing the hockey pucks prevents them from bouncing, so players can more easily control them. On average, 12 pucks are used in NHL games because they thaw so fast.
